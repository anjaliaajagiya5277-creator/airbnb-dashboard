This project presents an interactive Airbnb dashboard built using Tableau to analyze listing trends across different regions of New York City.

The dashboard provides insights into:
- Bed availability across top regions such as Manhattan, Brooklyn, and Queens
- Region-wise rating comparison to identify highly rated areas
- Average pricing trends over time (year-wise analysis)
- Geographic distribution of prices using zipcode-based mapping

The analysis highlights that Manhattan and Brooklyn dominate in terms of availability and pricing, while rating variations help identify customer-preferred locations.

This dashboard helps in understanding market trends, pricing strategies, and location-based performance in the Airbnb ecosystem.

Business Insights:
- Manhattan has the highest availability and pricing, indicating strong demand
- Brooklyn shows competitive pricing with high ratings
- Certain zipcodes have significantly higher prices, showing premium locations
- Price trends fluctuate over years, reflecting market dynamics
